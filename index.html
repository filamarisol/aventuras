Raúl Grigera was born on October 23th, 1886 in the neighborhood of Monsterrat, Buenos Aires, Argentina. Afro-Argentine, he was very popular during the decades of the 1920s and the 1930s. He died in 1955, in the mental institution 'Open Door' where he had been confined since 1941'. Alberto, Paulina. 'Títere roto”: vidas (posibles) y vidas póstumas del 'Negro Raúl', 2016

'...las caricaturas de Raúl en la historieta que salía en El Hogar en 1916 ('Las aventuras del Negro Raúl', de Lanteri) retratan, sin tapujos, a Raúl como un “mono con frac”, un “simulador” grotesco y patético (Gutiérrez, 1999: 75-76).'
